# Support

Towk is pre-1.0 community software. The public repository does not currently
provide an SLA, guaranteed response time, or commercial support contract.

- Use the bug form for reproducible Towk defects.
- Use the feature form for scoped product proposals.
- Use the question form for usage, deployment and self-hosting help.
- Use the private route in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) for vulnerabilities.

Open the appropriate form from the
[issue chooser](https://github.com/Yo-DDV/Towk/issues/new/choose). Before
submitting, remove secrets and personal data, search for duplicates and include
the exact Towk commit or image digest. General infrastructure administration and
unsupported third-party modifications may be redirected to their respective
projects.
